News

Major updates, announcements, and community milestones.

Network Expansion Planning

Our operations team has begun rolling out staged route expansion to support both Cityhopper and Mainline activity, with new medium-haul pairings expected to improve traffic flow and provide more rank-appropriate options for developing pilots. Dispatch balancing and event scheduling are being aligned so routes remain active and meaningful rather than duplicated.

ACARS & Pilot Experience Improvements

AviatesAir is refining the full pilot journey from signup through logging and analytics so members can clearly track progress, rank growth, and participation. While some dashboard telemetry is still in placeholder mode pending backend completion, the frontend structure now mirrors how live airline data will be surfaced when Cloudflare-connected services are finalized.

Community Training & Standards

Our training focus remains on realistic SOP-based flying, clean communication, and professional conduct in shared events. New pilots are encouraged to engage with experienced captains, participate in group operations, and maintain maturity throughout interactions. This ensures AviatesAir grows around quality and reliability rather than just raw flight volume.
