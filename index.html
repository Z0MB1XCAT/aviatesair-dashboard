About AviatesAir

AviatesAir is a community-driven virtual airline focused on realism, safe operations, and enjoyable group flying. We provide pilots with an ACARS client, scheduled events, and community support.

You can sign up to receive an ACARS key which authenticates your client to our systems and ties message history to your account.
