Pilot Information

How to start

Create an account, receive your ACARS key, and connect your simulator client to begin logging flights.

Operational standards

Use realistic routing, proper callsigns, and complete flight reports for every dispatched leg.

Subsidiaries

AviatesAir (mainline), AviatesAir Sky Cargo, and AviatesAir Cityhopper.

Community expectations

Be respectful, help new pilots, and contribute to a professional VA environment.
